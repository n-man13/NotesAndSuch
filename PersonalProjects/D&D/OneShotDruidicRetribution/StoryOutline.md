# One-shot: Draconic Druid Retribution

## Setting
Parlorreach is a small town located between Waterdeep and Daggerford, on the coastline of the world of Faerun.

## Starting point
The players start their adventure in a tavern near the edge of the town.

## Task from the lord of waterdeep
The players are tasked by the lord of waterdeep to bring some supplies to the misty forest elves in Issluna. The elves have faced a bad harvest.

## Beginning hook
While everyone is enjoying food and drinks at the tavern, a giant boar breaks down the southern wall of the tavern, starting a battle. A roaming elf druid, Elladan, arrives to help and explains that the bears originally are not overly hostile. They've been controlled by some kind of spell. The elves in the misty forest have faced similar attacks and can't figure out how to track the spell back to its source.

## If players don't have arcane knowledge
The players can complete the supply trip and get requested by the elves for a city defense mission, as owlbears start attacking.

## Finding the controller
If players decide to find the controller in Parlorreach, they can buy an Orb of Scrying for 25gp from an old arcane shopkeep. Alternatively, they can ask the church for a Cleric named Bethrynn to use divination and scrying to locate the caster.

## The caster
The caster is located under a small hill in The High Moor. Players will find a clearing with a giant elk and giant eagle guard near an unnatural cave.

## Dungeon encounters
* Lizard
* Owl
* Swarm of Bats
* Cave Bear

## Dungeon traps
* Tiles with animal faces are safe
* Flora and people faces unleash poisonous mushroom gas
* False floor with snake tracks in the dust, leading to a 10-foot drop onto wooden spikes
* Simple snare right after a door, easily noticeable

## Rooms
* Lizard and Owl in a small room with a water lake
* Tile trap hallway to a room with bat swarm and food storage
* False floor hallway to two rooms: left room is a library with books on dragon gods and Tiamat (the dragon), and right room has a snare inside the door with a cave bear sitting by a cold firepit, offering a place to rest for the party before the final boss

## Final boss
The final boss is a twisted evil druid Naga named Amithysee. She believes the world is too developed and that animals deserve to take back all the land. Her magic only imposes her thoughts on animals, like dominate beast. She has a level of 7 cleric but has never learned speak with animals and only learned a modified version of dominate beast with feeblemind attached.

Amithysee was taught by the Dark Lady (Tiamat). When fighting her, she doesn't use much druid magic and can't control her summoned green dragon wyrmling. She relies on cone magic from her mouth.

## Treasure
In a closet found in the last room, players can discover:
* Diary
* Chromatic dragon scales
* Ring of Spell Storing with 3 spell slots: one has the dominate feeble beast spell that BBEG used, and other 2 are empty.
